RIFT AI: Making Your Fridge Digital

A Raspberry Pi 5 system that uses computer vision to track what's in your fridge — built to run for days on end without babysitting.

The average household throws away roughly 30% of the food it buys. Most of the time, it's not because people don't care. It's because nobody actually knows what's sitting in the back of the fridge. RIFT AI started as a straightforward question: what if your fridge could just tell you?

The Hardware Setup

At the core of RIFT is a Raspberry Pi 5, chosen for its processing headroom and GPIO flexibility. A wide-angle camera module sits at the top of the enclosure, angled to cover the full interior shelf. A light sensor detects when the door opens, triggering a capture — no need to run vision inference continuously, which would kill the power budget.
