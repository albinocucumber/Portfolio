USC Solar Car — Chromoly Steel Space Frame Chassis

Leading chassis design for a competition solar car: from blank CAD file to a validated space frame that meets safety and weight targets simultaneously.

SolidWorks FEA Chromoly Steel Space Frame System Integration Team Lead
☀️

A solar car chassis has to do things that are almost contradictory: be light enough to preserve the power budget, stiff enough to protect the driver in a crash, and designed around a body shape that's dictated by aerodynamics. Getting all three right at the same time is the whole job.
